Team Fortress 2 has been my primary special interest since 2016. I have accumulated a variety of fan made characters over the course of those years, and my main passion project is re-telling the story with them. Which is the page you're viewing now!

This project isn't to completely rewrite the story, but to "remaster" it in a way. Keeping the original tale of the story, but adding new side plots, characters, events, and backstory. Seeing as Valve has currently left the series on a cliff hanger and not very consistent or clear, I figured I'd take matters into my own hands. Essentially, a massive expansion and retcon.

I'd recommend reading the "timeline" section, or reading the comics and updates from oldest to newest. If you have any more questions, check out the "F&Q" section!
