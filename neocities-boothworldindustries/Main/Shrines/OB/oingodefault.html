Oingo Boingo was a American band formed in 1979 by Danny Elfman. Originally, the group was known as "The Mystic Knights of Oingo Boingo" and was a musical theatre troupe group formed by Richard Elfman in 1972; before being taken by his brother.

Oingo Boingo has been heavily praised for its experimental music and high energy performances. Having shifted genres around from (In no particular order) ska, rock, jazz, punk, and art rock. They are typically referred to as "new wave". The group formally disbanded in 1995, with 8 studio albums and numerous film work under their belt.
