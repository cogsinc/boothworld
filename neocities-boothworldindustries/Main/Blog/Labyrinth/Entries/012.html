The more I think about it,
my love for one is not so far
from my hatred for another.
I hate the way you make me feel. I love the way you look at me.
I hate what you've done to my heart. I love your flaws and faults.
I hate what you make me say. I love what games we play.
I can't live without you, it becomes dull. But I can't stand to be in your presence, its too much.
I can't get enough either way.
All consuming like a
snake and its prey.
