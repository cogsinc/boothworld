Sites are rated "PG", "13", or "MA". Sites being two ratings such as "13-MA" means the site might have a page the visitor has to deliberately click on after a warning, or the site is unrated and subject to change. Please note the ratings are to be taken with a grain of salt, or being different then listed, as I can't verify every single little page someone has. Giving site ratings does not mean I think the site is "for kids" its just generally to give you an idea of what to expect. OK? (:]

Sites have 3 tags max, and are the "main theme". "Personal" has things like a blog/journal, "Art" includes drawings to physical items, "Writing" has things like poetry and fanfiction, "Fandom" includes things like shrines, "Music" is the webmasters own tunes, "Gaming" has things like mods or stats, "Coding" is resources or layouts, and "OCs" is focused on the webmasters original characters. Most are rather self explainatory.

I include websites here because I truly enjoy them, I am not very open to button exchanges unless we're friends. Please don't take it personal! And obligatory, I do not fully agree with every sentiment other webmasters have.
