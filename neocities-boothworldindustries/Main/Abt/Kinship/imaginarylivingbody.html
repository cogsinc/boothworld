This page is assuming you have a basic understanding of kin, I'll be honest in that I don't really feel like writing a whole essay on the concept. If you get it you get it, if you don't you don't. You can read more about kin and alterhumanity at the following pages: Alterhuman Dictionary, From Fiction. Both websites also link to more resources.

I kin metaphysically and psychologically, and no you don't get to know which are which >:P I consider my kin something very personal to me, so I'm not very open publicly about my more in-depth feelings about each kin; I do not engage in the community nor very interested in finding source mates. I have strong beliefs in the idea of past lives and also my sense of self. I don't mind people referring to these as "me" but personally I view my kintypes as just "parts" in understanding the larger system of myself.

I suppose in a way this page is a bit useless and more just for glamor or random trivia about myself. But really, what does it matter in the grander scheme of things if I do or don't? If I do, people spend their time gawking on just as useless things. And if I don't, I'm letting my own internal bully win. Does that make sense?

Mannequins

Mannequins are blank canvases meant to display beauty from the fabric they dawn on their plastic bodies. In the modern eye, many are unsettled by mannequins for such a human appearance. My form of mannequin is more on the "human face" side then the "blank white face" side, like the photo attached. I also associate jester masks and kirigumi masks to this kin. In a more derivative sense, I view this kin as something like "walking fabric" or something impersonating a human, but not supernatural or tragic in origin exactly. Just something watching, something playful and mute. An admirable avatar of silicon simultaneously of beauty while being exempt from humans.
Associated Places/Feelings: Department stores, glass shelves, fruity scents, smell of a closet, thrift stores.

Associated Music: Ulterior Motives, Supermodel, Hey Pretty, Jazzy Night, Liquid Smooth.

Associated Kin: Dora Smarmy (CB), Shaye Saint John, Puppet (FNAF), Francis Clara Censordoll (MO), Mafuyu Asahina (PSJK)

Ghosts are entities that have had many names across time and culture. But are commonly said to be visitors from the afterlife, or a mournful spirit unable to move onto peace. I personally feel more of the loneliness aspect of ghosts; haunting and observing and longing for humanity again. A sort of watcher and strange visitor you brought home with that vintage lamp you purchased. I always found it odd people believe ghosts to be violent or scary creatures, maybe if you just tried to understand them.
Associated Places/Feelings: Empty houses, angel statues, natural bodies of water, overgrown things, mirrors.

Associated Music: Dying Swan, Concertina Ballerina, Past Friends Re-United, You Are The Moon, Aphrodite of Death.

Associated Kin: Homura Akemi (MM), Mikage Souji, Anthy Himemiya (RGU), Emily (Corpse Bride), Puppet (FNAF), Grey Man (LSDDE)

Cogs/Robots

The Cogs are the antagonists of the MMO game "Toontown Online". The Cogs, in their original form, are business-men-like robots that're taking over Toontown by making the world grey and sad. They are split into four departments: Sellbot, Cashbot, Lawbot, and Bossbot; and all named after corporate terms like "Big Cheese" or "Yesmen". Since the original TTO has been taken offline, various new private servers have expanded upon the Cogs with new departments and character. I personally heavily drift towards Bossbot and some Lawbot. Although I am kin with robots and computers as a whole, Cogs are the best representation towards mine. Human-like but clearly off, sarcastic and blunt, and even sleazy with a dash of vintage corporate.
Associated Places/Feelings: Cityscapes, office software, 80s-90s office fashion, pre-2000s UI.

Associated Music: Private Life, Cool City, Sleaze, 4:00 AM, S.W.A.K., Cutting Edge. (Or anything by Luxury Elite in general)

Associated Kin: Flint Bonpyre, Thomas Saggs, Barry Brief, (TTCC), Club President (TTR), GLaDOS (Portal), Dora Smarmy (CB)

Zombies

Where zombies different from ghosts is their psychical presence. A corpse somehow reanimated without a motion of movement from the heart in their chest. My feeling of zombie isn't a mindless entity, but more of something in-between life and death; and something akin to a jester with a hint of tragedy. I would also say that I associate with "Frankenstein"-like creatures, I like to personally refer to them as "patchwork monsters". Deaths a big party, and everyones invited!

Associated Places/Feelings: Cold weather just before dark after its rained, stitching/patchwork, abandoned places.

Associated Music: Dead Man's Party, The Boogie Man, A Cold Freezin' Night, My Bloody Valentine, No One Lives Forever.

Associated Kin: Forsaken (WOW), Sally (TNBC), Renfield (Dracula), Mortality (TWF), Ghoulia Yelps (MH), Harry Warden (MBV)
