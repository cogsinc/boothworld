My strongest kintype would have to be a mixture of ghostkin, mannequinkin, and computerkin. Although they can be separate, they are heavily interwined for me. Some would probably consider this something like "conceptkin", but something like this: I am the ghost of a obsolete computer haunting a office building. A mannequin in a store window with no face but staring down. Something with a haunting but wasn't alive in the first place. Frequently I have felt like a observer among humans, something to gather information on their behaviors.

Mikage Souji
